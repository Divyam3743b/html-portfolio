BEST MOVIE ACCORDING TO DIVYAM

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Dragon Ball Z

It's my favorite anime series of all time.I like the action
and the story of this anime series.
The characters are well developed and the animation is top-notch.
The battles are intense and the transformations are epic.
I love how the characters grow and evolve throughout the series.

Jailer

It's a Tamil action thriller film directed by Nelson Dilipkumar.
The film stars Rajinikanth in the lead role, along with an ensemble cast.
The story revolves around a jailer who takes on a powerful gangster.
The film is known for its action sequences, music, and Rajinikanth's performance.
It has received positive reviews from critics and audiences alike.

Animal

It's a Hindi action thriller film directed by Sandeep Reddy Vanga.
The film stars Ranbir Kapoor in the lead role, along with an ensemble cast.
The story revolves around a gangster who is on a mission to avenge his father's death.
The film is known for its intense action sequences, music, and Ranbir Kapoor's performance.
It has received positive reviews from critics and audiences alike.
